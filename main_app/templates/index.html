Simplihive

Simplify your life with the help of your hive

Decluttering can be hard because we naturally form attachments to items in our lives. With Simplihive, you post items you are having trouble parting ways with, write a brief description, and describe what significance the item holds. You can even upload a photo of the item. Then, your community hive will vote whether you should donate, keep, or trash the item.
